## The Structural Trap of Mass Ecosystems
Mainstream wellness ecosystems operate on a "Master-Slave" terminal model. Your smartphone gathers data, packages it as a payload, and funnels it into centralized cloud servers managed by massive technology conglomerates. 

In this structure, you do not own your health metrics; you merely rent access to the insights derived from them. Even if an application promises end-to-end encryption, the underlying operating system itself remains an active telemetry collection engine, logging location data, network interactions, and device identifiers in the background.

To break free from this model, an alternative must do more than just host your data securely—it must fundamentally alter how that data is processed.
