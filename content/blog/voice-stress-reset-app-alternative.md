1. **Acoustic Surveillance:** You are transmitting unencrypted behavioral audio files containing the specific cadence, tone, and emotional undertones of your voice to third-party corporate servers.
2. **Psychological Profiling:** By matching vocal frequencies to acute stress states over time, central cloud databases compile an intimate psychological profile of your emotional stability, stress thresholds, and cognitive fatigue.
3. **Latency Inefficiency:** Relying on round-trip cloud computing introduces processing delays that disrupt the immediate, real-time feedback loop required to effectively train and reset autonomic nervous system responses.
